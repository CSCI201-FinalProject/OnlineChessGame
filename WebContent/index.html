What is Elo?

The Elo rating system is a method for calculating the relative skill levels of players. The performance rating is determined by the "algorithm of 400". The algorithm determines the probability of one player beating another. Then raises or lowers the winners or losers Elo respectively by this probability.

In case you are wondering our Elo formulas are as follows:


Probability of winning = 1.0 / (1.0 + 10^( (P1 - P2) / 400)))

winning_player_elo_gain = (100/tg) * (1.0 - WPp)

losing_player_elo_loss = (100/tg) * (1.0 - LPp)

Where:

tg = total_games

LPp = losing_player_win_probability

WPp = winning_player_win_probability

P1 = player1_elo

P2 = player2_elo
